Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

At BhavyaCare, your privacy and data security are our top priorities. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal and medical information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, mobile number, email address, and physical address/pincode.
  • Medical Information: Medical prescriptions, lab test reports, and symptoms provided during doctor consultations.
  • Financial Information: Payment screenshots and transaction IDs (UPI/Bank) for order verification.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To facilitate bookings for lab tests, doctor consultations, and pharmacy orders.
  • To manage your VIP Family Plan and BhavyaCare Wallet.
  • To communicate with you regarding appointments, test results, and order updates.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. Your information is shared only with the specific third-party provider (Doctor, Lab, or Pharmacy) assigned to fulfill your requested service. For example, your address and prescription are shared with the pharmacy for home delivery.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures, including encryption and secure server hosting, to protect your personal and medical records from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access your personal data, request corrections, and ask for the deletion of your medical records from our active databases, subject to legal and regulatory retention requirements.
