Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

BhavyaCare strives to ensure a seamless healthcare experience. If you need to cancel a booking, please review our cancellation and refund guidelines below.

1. Doctor Consultations

  • Cancellation by Patient: Online or offline consultations can be cancelled prior to the scheduled slot. If paid online, a full refund will be initiated.
  • Doctor No-Show/Unavailable: If the doctor cancels the appointment or is unavailable, you are entitled to a 100% refund.

2. Lab Tests & Profiles

  • Cancellation Before Sample Collection: You can cancel your lab test order anytime before the phlebotomist arrives for home collection or before you visit the lab center. A full refund will be provided for prepaid orders.
  • After Sample Collection: No cancellations or refunds are permitted once the sample has been collected.

3. Pharmacy Orders

  • Orders can be cancelled before the pharmacy confirms and dispatches the medicines.
  • Once the order status changes to "Confirmed" or is out for delivery, cancellation may not be possible. Returns of delivered medicines are subject to the individual pharmacy's return policy.

4. Refund Methods & Timelines

When claiming a refund through your dashboard, you can choose between two methods:

1. BhavyaCare Wallet (Recommended): Refunds are credited instantly to your platform wallet and can be used for future bookings.

2. Bank/UPI Account: If you choose a bank refund, the amount will be processed manually. As per our financial cycle, bank refunds take time and are typically processed on the 1st of the next month.

5. VIP Membership

VIP Family Plan membership fees are non-refundable once activated and utilized for any discounted booking on the platform.
